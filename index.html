Aims

We aim to increase understanding about priority-setting in healthcare. We achieve this in the following ways:

  1. Building models
  2. Sharing our work
  3. Reviewing other models

Sharing our work

You can find our GitHub repository at github.com/ForecastHealth. Our codebase is currently private, but check back for regular updates.

Reviewing other models

We are always interested to review your, or other groups' models. Our general approach to this is follows:

  • review the original model with the original assumptions
  • review the original model with modified assumptions
  • review the re-written* model with original assumptions
  • review the re-written* model with modified assumptions

*We re-write the model using our codebase and engine

Contact
